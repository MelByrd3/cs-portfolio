NIKE

Nike was founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight in the mid 1960's orginally as Blue Ribbon Sports until the change of their name in 1971. Now being known Nike, these two entrepreneurs set their goals on being at the top, not only with world renouned shoes like the Air Jordan Series, the foamposite series, and multiple running shoe types. Nike is at the top of the basketball shoe pyrimid, earning 27.8 billion dollars in revenue world wide, and also supporting players in the NBA, NFL, MLB, and other major sport assosiations by partnering up with outstanding players, and giving them their own brand shoe. A list of players who have done that can include Lebron James, Kobe Bryant, Kevin Durant, and even Kyrie Irving.
