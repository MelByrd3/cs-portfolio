A BATHING APE

A BATHING APE was a japanese luxury street closthing brand created by NIGO, who works as a DJ, fashion entrepreneur, and producer. The BAPE look spread amongst the teenage community in Japan, and this made NIGO's small shop of litte variations of clothing, into a well known, world wide clothing company. NIGO has won an a Japanese MTV style award for his clothing line, and till this day still makes much profit off of the clothing that was made for the individuals loyal to the street.
