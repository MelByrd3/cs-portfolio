VINDINUM

Vindinum is an AI progamming game that has you controlling your own Artifical Intelligence character, as you collect mines, regenerate on health, and attack other Artifical Intelligence characters controlled by other users. The goal of the game is to make sure that your AI has the most gold by the game's end, and the only way to claim coins, is by either taking free mines, or also by stealing enemy mines. Since this is a turned based game, the 1200 moves are shared amongst all 4 AI characters, each character having 300 moves.
