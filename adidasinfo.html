ADIDAS

Adolf Dassler the creator of the famous German Triple-Stripe, was born on Novermber 3rd, 1900 in Herzogenaurach, Germany. This man is apart of the Dassler family, which means that he was apart of the biggest sneaker family to ever live, with Adolf's younger brother being Rudolf Dassler, the creator of another major fashion and sneaker company named Puma. Adolf Dassler and his younger brother were both innovators in their time, and both brothers made a lasting impression that last till this day.
